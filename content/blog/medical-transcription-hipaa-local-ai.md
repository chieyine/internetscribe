Medical dictation is a huge part of a doctor's life. But traditional services are expensive, and cloud tools can be a HIPAA minefield.

## The HIPAA Challenge

To be HIPAA compliant, a cloud service needs a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) and strict security controls. Most free online tools do NOT offer this.

## Local AI = Inherently Secure

Because **InternetScribe** processes data on your device, it's much easier to maintain compliance. You aren't transmitting PHI (Protected Health Information) across the internet.

- **Note:** Always consult your compliance officer. But generally, processing data on a secure, encrypted laptop is safer than uploading it to a random website.

## Accuracy for Medical Terms

Whisper is surprisingly good at medical jargon, though specialized models (like Med-PaLM) are better. For general notes and patient letters, it's a massive time saver.
