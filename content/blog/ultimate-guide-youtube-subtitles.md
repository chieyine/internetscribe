## SRT (SubRip)

The classic format. Simple text with timestamps. Supported by almost everything.

## VTT (WebVTT)

The modern web standard. Supports styling and positioning. Best for HTML5 video players.

## How to Create Them

**InternetScribe** exports both.

1.  Transcribe your video.
2.  Click "Export".
3.  Choose "VTT" for web or "SRT" for older players.
