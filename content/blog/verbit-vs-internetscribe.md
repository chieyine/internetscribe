Verbit is a massive player in the legal and educational transcription market. They offer SLAs, 99.9% accuracy guarantees, and specialized compliance.

**InternetScribe** is not an enterprise platform. It's a tool for individuals.

## The "Shadow IT" Use Case

However, employees in enterprises often need quick transcripts _now_. They don't want to go through procurement to get a Verbit account.

**InternetScribe** is perfect for this "ad-hoc" usage. Because it's private and local, it doesn't violate data exfiltration policies (check with your IT department, of course).

## Conclusion

Verbit is for the organization. **InternetScribe** is for the employee.
